About Us

KI-LABS We desire to supplement the learning of students from just theoretical into complete and overall learning by offering student centric practical cum live experimental classes right at your door step. We would like to extend this good opportunity to offer state of the art, class wise and month wise practical experiments under highly trained and experienced tutors. Our primary aim is to harness the talent of the children rather just demonstration. We are looking forward to joining hands with you in harnessing the budding talents in your Students.
